Wave Raiders is a 2-player competitive game where two viking ships fight each other on the rolling waves of the sea, sky, and SPACE!

Controls:

Red Boat:
A/D - Move left and right
W - Fire lob cannon
S - Fire standard cannon
X - Fire torpedo

Blue Boat:
Left Arrow/Right Arrow - Move left and right
Enter Key - Fire lob cannon
Up Arrow - Fire standard cannon
Down Arrow - Fire torpedo
